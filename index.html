Philanthropy. Investments. Ventures.

Welcome to the family office of Sanjay Krishna Goyal

We are a private family office founded in 2022. Our objective is to protect and grow our financial capital as well as support social and economic ventures which benefit all stakeholders and the society at large.

Our focus is not just on growing our assets but on being a force for positive change. We wish to align our actions and investments with the values that define us and guide us towards creating a better future for everyone.

Philanthropy

Philanthropy reflects a conscious effort to address the root causes of social issues, foster equity and create long-term, sustainable impact. The SKG Family Office believes in empowering communities, supporting innovation in areas like education, health and environment. In an increasingly interconnected society, we would like to play a vital role in building resilience, promoting social cohesion, and shaping a future where opportunity and dignity are accessible to all.

Investment

Our investment portfolio is built neither to minimize risk nor to maximize returns but to perform well in all market conditions and provide superior returns consistently. We call it the “weather” approach to investing (a phrase coined and popularized by Ray Dalio).
